Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

BlinkAlarm does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or images. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Camera Usage

BlinkAlarm uses the front-facing camera solely for real-time blink detection through Apple's Vision framework. The camera feed is processed frame-by-frame on your device and is never recorded, saved, or transmitted to any server.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Blink session records (start time, duration, blink count, average blink rate)
  • User preferences (selected theme, alert settings, language preference)

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible to the BlinkAlarm app. You can delete all session data at any time by deleting the app.

No Data Collection

BlinkAlarm does not:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Record or store any images or video
  • Use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use cookies or any tracking technologies

No Third-Party Services

BlinkAlarm does not integrate with any third-party services. The app operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

Children's Privacy

BlinkAlarm does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
